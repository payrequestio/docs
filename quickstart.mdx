# Quick Start Guide

Get started with PayRequest and create your first invoice in just a few steps. This guide will walk you through setting up your account and sending your first professional invoice.

## Step 1: Create Your Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up for PayRequest">
    Visit [dashboard.payrequest.io](https://dashboard.payrequest.io) and create your free account. You'll get instant access to create and send invoices.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up your business profile">
    Navigate to **Settings > Business Profile** in your dashboard to add your company information, logo, and payment details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose how you want to get paid">
    Configure your preferred payment methods and bank account information where you want to receive payments:
    - **Bank Transfer** - Direct deposits to your bank account
    - **Credit Cards** - Accept Visa, MasterCard, and other major cards
    - **Digital Wallets** - PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and more
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 2: Create Your First Invoice

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Dashboard Invoice Creation">
    The easiest way to create professional invoices:

    1. Go to **Invoices > Create Invoice** in your dashboard
    2. Fill in the invoice details:
       - **Customer Information**: Name, email, and billing address
       - **Invoice Items**: Description, quantity, and price for each item/service
       - **Payment Terms**: Due date and payment instructions
       - **Branding**: Add your logo and customize colors
    3. Preview your invoice to ensure it looks professional
    4. Click **Send Invoice** to email it directly to your customer

    Your customer will receive a beautifully formatted invoice with a secure payment link where they can pay instantly.
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Quick Payment Link">
    For simple one-off payments:

    1. Go to **Payments > Create Payment Link** in your dashboard
    2. Fill in the payment details (amount, description, recipient email)
    3. Click **Create Payment Link**
    4. Share the generated link with your customer

    ```
    https://payrequest.io/p/your-payment-id
    ```

    Your customer can pay instantly using this link, and you'll receive real-time notifications.
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Recurring Invoices">
    For subscription or recurring billing:

    1. Create an invoice as described above
    2. In the invoice settings, enable **Recurring Invoice**
    3. Set your billing frequency (weekly, monthly, quarterly, yearly)
    4. Configure automatic sending and payment processing
    5. Save the recurring invoice template

    PayRequest will automatically generate and send invoices according to your schedule, and process payments when customers pay.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Step 3: Track Payments and Manage Customers

Stay on top of your invoices and payments:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Payment Tracking" icon="chart-line">
    Monitor invoice status in real-time:
    - **Sent**: Invoice has been emailed to customer
    - **Viewed**: Customer has opened the invoice
    - **Paid**: Payment has been completed
    - **Overdue**: Invoice is past due date
  </Card>
  <Card title="Customer Management" icon="users">
    Build and maintain your customer database:
    - Store customer contact information
    - Track payment history and preferences
    - Set up automatic payment reminders
    - Export customer data for accounting
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Payment Notifications

You'll receive instant notifications when:
- A customer views your invoice
- A payment is completed
- An invoice becomes overdue
- A payment fails or is disputed

Configure notifications in **Settings > Notifications** to choose how you want to be alerted (email, SMS, or dashboard only).
