# Welcome to PayRequest 🚀

PayRequest is a comprehensive billing automation platform designed to streamline your entire payment workflow. Whether you're running a SaaS business, selling digital products, or managing recurring subscriptions, PayRequest provides all the tools you need to automate your billing processes and grow your business.

## What is PayRequest?

PayRequest transforms complex billing operations into simple, automated workflows. Our platform handles everything from product creation and customer management to payment processing and financial reporting - all from one centralized dashboard.

## Why Choose PayRequest?

### 🎯 Built for Modern Businesses
PayRequest is designed specifically for digital businesses that need flexible billing solutions. From seat-based SaaS pricing to one-time product sales, our platform adapts to your business model.

### 🔐 Secure & Compliant
We prioritize security with PCI compliance, GDPR compliance, and secure payment processing through trusted providers like Mollie and Stripe.

### 🌍 European-First Approach
With native support for SEPA Direct Debit, iDEAL, Bancontact, and other European payment methods, PayRequest is built for the European market.

### ⚡ Automation-Powered
Reduce manual work with powerful automation tools:
- Automatic subscription billing
- Smart email notifications
- Tag-based customer segmentation
- Failed payment recovery
- Revenue reporting and analytics
