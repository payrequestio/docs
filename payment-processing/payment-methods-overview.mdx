## 🏦 European Bank Payment Methods

Optimized for European customers with local banking preferences and lower processing costs.

### iDEAL (Netherlands)

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="🇳🇱 iDEAL - Netherlands' Leading Payment Method">
    **Market Dominance**: Used by 60%+ of Dutch online shoppers
    **Bank Coverage**: All major Dutch banks supported
    **Security**: Bank-level authentication and authorization
    **Customer Experience**: Direct bank-to-bank transfer with instant confirmation

    **How it Works:**
    1. Customer selects iDEAL at checkout
    2. Chooses their bank from a list
    3. Redirected to secure bank environment
    4. Authenticates using bank credentials
    5. Confirms payment amount and details
    6. Returns to merchant with payment confirmation

    **Business Benefits:**
    - Lower transaction fees than cards
    - Guaranteed payment (no chargebacks)
    - Instant payment confirmation
    - High customer trust and adoption
    - No setup fees or monthly costs
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="💰 iDEAL Pricing and Costs">
    **Transaction Fees**: Typically €0.29 per transaction
    **No Percentage Fee**: Fixed cost regardless of amount
    **No Setup Costs**: Free to activate through PayRequest
    **Volume Discounts**: Available for high-transaction merchants

    **Cost Comparison Example:**
    - €100 payment via iDEAL: €0.29 fee (0.29%)
    - €100 payment via credit card: €2.50+ fee (2.5%+)
    - €1000 payment via iDEAL: €0.29 fee (0.029%)
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Bancontact (Belgium)

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="🇧🇪 Bancontact - Belgium's National Payment Scheme">
    **Market Share**: 70%+ of Belgian online payments
    **Bank Integration**: All Belgian banks participate
    **Mobile App**: Bancontact by Payconiq for mobile payments
    **QR Code Support**: In-store and online QR payments

    **Customer Flow:**
    1. Select Bancontact at checkout
    2. Enter card number or use mobile app
    3. Authenticate with card reader or mobile PIN
    4. Confirm payment in banking environment
    5. Instant payment confirmation

    **Advantages:**
    - Trusted by Belgian consumers
    - Lower costs than international cards
    - Instant payment processing
    - Mobile-first innovation
    - Fraud protection through bank security
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### SEPA Direct Debit

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="🏦 SEPA Direct Debit - European Recurring Payments">
    **Coverage**: All 36 SEPA countries (EU + EEA + others)
    **Use Cases**: Perfect for subscriptions and recurring billing
    **Mandate System**: Customer authorization for automatic payments
    **Settlement**: T+1 settlement (next business day)

    **SEPA Direct Debit Process:**
    1. Customer provides mandate authorization
    2. Merchant collects signed mandate
    3. First payment processed with advance notice
    4. Subsequent payments automatically debited
    5. Customer retains refund rights (8 weeks)

    **Business Benefits:**
    - Lowest cost payment method in Europe
    - Automatic recurring billing
    - High success rates
    - Predictable cash flow
    - Reduced payment friction for customers

    **Requirements:**
    - Valid SEPA mandate from customer
    - 14-day advance notice for first payment
    - 5-day notice for subsequent payments
    - Creditor identifier (provided by PayRequest)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="📋 SEPA Mandate Management">
    **Digital Mandates**: Electronic signature collection
    **Mandate Storage**: Secure, compliant mandate archiving
    **Customer Communication**: Automated mandate notifications
    **Compliance**: Full SEPA regulation adherence

    **Mandate Types:**
    - **One-off**: Single payment authorization
    - **Recurring**: Multiple payment authorization
    - **Business-to-Business**: B2B specific mandates with shorter refund periods

    **PayRequest Automation:**
    - Automatic mandate creation during checkout
    - Digital signature collection
    - Mandate status tracking and management
    - Automatic pre-notification emails
    - Failed payment retry logic
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Bank Transfers

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="🏛️ Automatic Bank Transfer Matching">
    **Zero Fee Payments**: Accept bank transfers with no PayRequest transaction fees
    **Automatic Matching**: Ponto integration matches incoming payments automatically
    **Unique References**: Each payment gets a unique reference code (PAY-12345)
    **Instant Recognition**: Payments matched within minutes of arriving in your bank

    **How It Works:**
    1. Customer selects Bank Transfer at checkout
    2. Customer sees your IBAN and unique reference code
    3. Customer transfers exact amount via their bank
    4. Ponto syncs your bank transactions automatically
    5. PayRequest matches the reference and marks invoice as paid

    **Requirements:**
    - Ponto bank connection (Settings → Ponto)
    - Business bank account with Ponto-supported bank
    - Bank Transfer payment method enabled

    **Use Cases:**
    - High-value B2B transactions
    - Cost-conscious customers (0% fees)
    - Customers preferring bank-direct payments
    - Markets with strong bank transfer culture (Netherlands, Belgium, Germany)

    **PayRequest Features:**
    - Automatic bank details display with copy buttons
    - Unique payment reference generation
    - Automatic payment matching via Ponto
    - Real-time bank transaction syncing
    - No manual reconciliation needed

    [Learn more about Bank Transfer Payments →](/payment-processing/bank-transfer)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="🌐 International Bank Transfers">
    **SWIFT Network**: International bank transfers for global payments
    **Manual Process**: Customer-initiated transfers without automatic matching
    **Settlement Time**: 3-5 business days for international transfers

    **Use Cases:**
    - International customers outside SEPA zone
    - High-value cross-border transactions
    - Customers without access to other payment methods
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
