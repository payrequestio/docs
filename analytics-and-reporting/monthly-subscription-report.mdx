# Monthly Subscription Report 📊

Stay on top of your subscription business with automated monthly email reports delivered straight to your inbox. Get a comprehensive overview of your subscription performance, revenue trends, customer behavior, and actionable insights - all without lifting a finger.

## What Is the Monthly Subscription Report?

The Monthly Subscription Report is an automated email summary that provides a complete retrospective analysis of your subscription business performance. Every month, you'll receive a beautifully designed email showcasing your key metrics, growth patterns, and areas that need attention.

<Tip>
The report is sent on **the 1st of each month at 8:00 AM**, covering the previous month's data. For example, on November 1st, you'll receive insights for October.
</Tip>

## What's Included in Your Report

### 🎯 Business Overview

Get instant visibility into your subscription health with key metrics:

- **Active Subscriptions**: Total number of paying subscribers at month-end
- **New Subscriptions**: Customers who started subscriptions during the month
- **Reactivated Subscriptions**: Customers who resumed paused or canceled subscriptions
- **Cancelled Subscriptions**: Customers who ended their subscriptions
- **Total Revenue**: Your Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR) for the period
- **Average Revenue Per User (ARPU)**: The average value of each subscription

### 🔄 Renewals & Billing Performance

Monitor the health of your billing system with detailed renewal statistics:

- **Renewal Attempts**: Total number of subscription renewals processed
- **Successful Renewals**: How many renewals completed successfully
- **Success Rate**: Percentage of successful renewals (with performance rating)
- **Failed Payments**: Number of renewal failures that need attention

<Tip>
A renewal success rate above 95% is excellent! If you're seeing lower rates, the report will provide specific recommendations to improve payment success.
</Tip>

### 💳 Chargebacks & Refunds

Keep track of payment disputes and customer refunds:

- **Chargebacks**: Number and total value of payment disputes from customers
- **Refunds**: Number and total value of refunds processed
- **Total Impact**: Combined financial impact on your business
- **Smart Insights**: Automatic warnings when chargeback rates exceed healthy levels

The report provides intelligent recommendations based on your chargeback volume:

- ✅ **No issues**: Congratulations when you have zero chargebacks
- 👍 **Under control**: When impact is minimal (< €100)
- ⚠️ **Monitor closely**: When you should review patterns (€100-500)
- 🚨 **Action required**: When high volumes need immediate attention (> €500)

### 🏆 Top 5 Most Popular Subscriptions

Discover which subscription products are driving your business:

- **Product Rankings**: Your five best-performing subscription offerings
- **Active Subscriber Count**: How many customers have each subscription
- **Monthly Revenue**: The recurring revenue generated by each product

Use this insight to:
- Focus marketing efforts on popular products
- Identify underperforming offerings
- Make informed pricing decisions
- Plan product development priorities

### 📈 Strategic Insights

Automatically calculated key performance indicators help you understand your business trajectory:

#### Growth Metrics
- **Growth Rate**: How fast your subscriber base is expanding
- **Churn Rate**: Percentage of customers canceling subscriptions
- **Payment Success Rate**: Overall billing reliability

#### Performance Benchmarks
- **Chargeback Rate**: Percentage of renewals resulting in disputes
- **Industry Comparisons**: See how your metrics compare to healthy benchmarks

<Warning>
The report includes intelligent recommendations when metrics fall outside healthy ranges, helping you identify and address issues before they impact revenue.
</Warning>

## Enabling Monthly Reports

Monthly Subscription Reports are controlled through your notification settings:

1. Navigate to **Settings** in your dashboard
2. Click on the **Notifications** tab
3. Scroll to the **Subscriptions** section
4. Toggle **"Monthly Subscription Report"** to ON

<Tip>
You can enable or disable these reports at any time. Your setting will take effect for the next scheduled report.
</Tip>

## Report Delivery Schedule

### When You'll Receive Reports

- **Delivery Date**: 1st of every month
- **Delivery Time**: 8:00 AM (local time)
- **Reporting Period**: Previous full calendar month

## Understanding Your Data

### What Counts in the Report

The monthly report includes:

- **Active Subscriptions**: All subscriptions in "active" status on the last day of the month
- **New Subscriptions**: Subscriptions created during the reporting period
- **Completed Renewals**: Successful recurring payments processed
- **Revenue**: Only from successfully completed payments

### Data Accuracy

- **Real-Time Collection**: Data is gathered throughout the month as events occur
- **Final Snapshot**: Reports reflect the complete month with no partial data
- **Transaction-Based**: All metrics calculated from actual payment transactions
- **Multi-Currency Support**: Amounts displayed in your configured currency

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What if I don't have any subscriptions?">
    You'll still receive the report, but it will show zeros for subscription metrics. This is useful for tracking when you launch your subscription business and seeing your first metrics appear.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I receive reports for multiple businesses?">
    Each PayRequest account receives its own monthly report. If you manage multiple businesses, you'll receive a separate report for each one.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Will I receive a report if I just signed up?">
    Yes! You'll receive your first report on the 1st of the month following your signup. The report will cover whatever data you have from the partial month.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I change the email address that receives reports?">
    Reports are sent to your primary account email. Update your email address in **Settings → Personal** to change where reports are delivered.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if I miss a report?">
    Reports are sent once per month. If you miss one, you can view your subscription data in your dashboard at any time by visiting **Subscriptions** and using the date filters.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I get reports more frequently?">
    Yes! Enable the **Daily Business Summary** for a morning overview of the last 24 hours, including overnight billing results, payments, and new customers. Go to **Settings → Notifications → Reports & Digests** to enable it. See the [Daily Business Summary](/analytics-and-reporting/daily-business-summary) documentation for details.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Why did I receive a report about chargebacks?">
    The report automatically includes chargeback data when relevant. This helps you stay informed about payment disputes so you can take action to reduce them and protect your revenue.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
