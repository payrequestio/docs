## Real-Time Payment Notifications

Stay informed with instant notifications:

### Notification Types

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Email Notifications">
    Receive emails when:
    - Invoice is viewed by customer
    - Payment is completed
    - Payment fails or is declined
    - Invoice becomes overdue
    - Customer requests changes
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="SMS Notifications">
    Get text messages for:
    - High-value payments completed
    - Urgent overdue notices
    - Failed payment attempts
    - Customer payment confirmations
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Dashboard Notifications">
    See activity in your dashboard:
    - Real-time activity feed
    - Payment status updates
    - Customer interaction timeline
    - System alerts and notices
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Notification Settings
Customize what notifications you receive:

1. Go to **Settings > Notifications**
2. Choose notification methods (email, SMS, dashboard)
3. Select which events trigger notifications
4. Set quiet hours for non-urgent notifications

## Overdue Invoice Management

### Identifying Overdue Payments

Quickly spot invoices that need attention:

- **Overdue Filter**: View all past-due invoices
- **Aging Buckets**: Sort by 1-30, 31-60, 60+ days overdue
- **Customer Risk Scores**: Identify consistently late payers
- **Priority Rankings**: Focus on high-value overdue invoices

### Automated Follow-ups

Set up automatic reminder sequences:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Pre-Due Reminders">
    Send friendly reminders 3-5 days before due date
  </Step>
  <Step title="Due Date Notices">
    Notify customers on the due date
  </Step>
  <Step title="Overdue Escalation">
    Increase urgency with each follow-up
  </Step>
  <Step title="Final Notices">
    Send final payment demands before collections
  </Step>
</Steps>
