## Bank Transfers

### Direct Bank Transfers
Lower cost option for larger payments:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="ACH Transfers (US)" icon="building-columns">
    - Lower processing fees
    - 2-3 business day settlement
    - Perfect for recurring payments
    - Reduced chargeback risk
  </Card>
  <Card title="SEPA Transfers (Europe)" icon="euro-sign">
    - Euro-denominated transfers
    - Same-day or next-day settlement
    - Low cost for European customers
    - Bank-to-bank security
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Wire Transfers
For high-value international payments:
- Secure bank-to-bank transfers
- Same-day processing available
- Higher fees but immediate settlement
- Full payment traceability

## Payment Processing Fees

### Understanding Payment Costs

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Card Payments" icon="credit-card">
    - Visa/MasterCard: 2.9% + $0.30
    - American Express: 3.4% + $0.30
    - International cards: +1% extra
    - No monthly fees
  </Card>
  <Card title="Bank Transfers" icon="building-columns">
    - ACH transfers: 1% (max $10)
    - Wire transfers: $15 flat fee
    - SEPA transfers: €0.35 flat fee
    - No percentage fees
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Fee Optimization Tips
<Tip>
  Encourage bank transfers for larger payments to reduce processing fees and improve your margins.
</Tip>

- Offer bank transfer discounts for large invoices
- Set minimum amounts for credit card payments
- Consider passing fees to customers for small payments
- Use ACH for recurring payments when possible

## Best Practices

### Optimizing Payment Success
- **Offer Multiple Options**: Give customers choice in payment methods
- **Local Preferences**: Enable regional payment methods for international customers
- **Clear Pricing**: Display all fees transparently
- **Mobile Optimization**: Ensure all payment methods work on mobile devices

### Improving Customer Experience
- **One-Click Payments**: Enable saved payment methods for returning customers
- **Guest Checkout**: Allow payments without account creation
- **Payment Confirmation**: Send immediate payment confirmations
- **Receipt Management**: Provide easy access to payment receipts
