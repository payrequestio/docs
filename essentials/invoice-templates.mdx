# Invoice Templates

Save time and ensure consistency by creating reusable invoice templates for your common services and products. Templates help you create professional invoices quickly while maintaining your brand standards.

## Creating Templates

### From Existing Invoices
The easiest way to create templates:

1. Create a complete invoice with all details
2. Click **Save as Template** before sending
3. Name your template descriptively
4. Set default payment terms and due dates
5. Choose what information to pre-populate

### Template Builder
Create templates from scratch:

- **Template Name**: Descriptive name for easy identification
- **Default Items**: Standard line items with descriptions and pricing
- **Payment Terms**: Default due dates and payment conditions
- **Branding**: Logo, colors, and custom messaging
- **Customer Fields**: Which customer information to include

## Managing Templates

### Template Organization
Keep your templates organized:

- **Categories**: Group by service type or customer segment
- **Tags**: Add searchable tags for quick filtering
- **Favorites**: Mark frequently used templates
- **Archiving**: Hide outdated templates without deleting
