## Security Best Practices

### For Your Business
<Tip>
  Implement these security practices to protect your business and customers.
</Tip>

**Account Management:**
- Use strong, unique passwords for all accounts
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)
- Regularly review user access and permissions
- Monitor account activity for suspicious behavior

**Invoice Security:**
- Include clear terms and conditions
- Use secure payment links only
- Verify customer email addresses
- Monitor for fraudulent payment attempts

### For Your Customers
Help your customers stay secure:

**Payment Best Practices:**
- Always use secure payment pages (HTTPS)
- Never share payment information via email
- Verify invoice authenticity before paying
- Keep payment receipts for records

**Communication Security:**
- Use official PayRequest domains only
- Verify suspicious communications directly
- Report potential phishing attempts
- Educate customers about payment security

If you discover a security issue:

1. **Do not disclose publicly** - Contact us directly first
2. **Email security@payrequest.io** with details
3. **Include steps to reproduce** if applicable
4. **We'll respond within 24 hours** to acknowledge receipt
5. **Updates provided** throughout investigation
