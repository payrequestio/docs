## Automated Reporting

### Scheduled Reports
Set up automatic report generation:

- **Daily Summaries**: Quick overview of daily activity
- **Weekly Performance**: Weekly business metrics
- **Monthly Financials**: Comprehensive monthly reports
- **Quarterly Reviews**: Detailed quarterly analysis

### Report Delivery
Choose how to receive your reports:

- **API Access**: Programmatic access to report data

## Best Practices

### Regular Review Schedule
<Tip>
  Establish a regular reporting schedule to stay on top of your business performance and identify trends early.
</Tip>

- **Daily**: Check cash flow and payment activity
- **Weekly**: Review overdue invoices and customer issues
- **Monthly**: Analyze overall business performance
- **Quarterly**: Strategic planning and goal assessment
