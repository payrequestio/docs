# Creating Invoices

PayRequest makes it easy to create professional, branded invoices that get you paid faster. Whether you need to bill for services, products, or time, our invoice builder has everything you need.

## Getting Started with Invoices

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access the Invoice Builder">
    Navigate to **Invoices > Create New Invoice** in your PayRequest dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Customer Information">
    Enter your customer's details including name, email, and billing address.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Invoice Items">
    List the products or services you're billing for with quantities and prices.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize and Send">
    Add your branding, set payment terms, and send the invoice.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Invoice Builder Features

### Customer Information
Easily manage customer details:
- **Customer Database**: Select from existing customers or add new ones
- **Billing Address**: Include complete billing information
- **Contact Details**: Email and phone number for communication
- **Customer Notes**: Add internal notes about the customer

### Invoice Items and Pricing
Build detailed invoices with:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Line Items" icon="list">
    - Product/service descriptions
    - Quantity and unit prices
    - Tax rates and calculations
    - Discounts and adjustments
  </Card>
  <Card title="Pricing Options" icon="dollar-sign">
    - Multiple currencies supported
    - Automatic tax calculations
    - Percentage or fixed discounts
    - Shipping and handling fees
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Branding and Customization

Make your invoices look professional:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Visual Branding">
    - Upload your company logo
    - Choose brand colors
    - Select from professional templates
    - Customize header and footer text
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Content Customization">
    - Add custom invoice numbers
    - Include payment terms and conditions
    - Add notes and special instructions
    - Include your business registration details
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Invoice Templates

Save time by creating reusable templates:

### Creating Templates
1. Build an invoice with your standard items and pricing
2. Click **Save as Template** before sending
3. Name your template (e.g., "Monthly Consulting", "Website Design")
4. Set default payment terms and due dates

### Using Templates
- Select a template when creating new invoices
- Customize details for each customer
- Templates automatically include your branding
- Perfect for recurring services or standard packages

## Payment Terms and Due Dates

Configure how and when you want to be paid:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Payment Terms" icon="calendar">
    Set clear expectations:
    - Net 15, 30, or 60 days
    - Due on receipt
    - Custom payment schedules
    - Early payment discounts
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Due Date Options" icon="clock">
    Choose what works for your business:
    - Fixed due dates
    - Days from invoice date
    - End of month terms
    - Custom due date calculations
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

Multiple ways to deliver your invoices:

### Email Delivery
- Professional email templates
- Automatic delivery confirmation
- Include payment instructions
- Add personal messages

### Direct Sharing

## Best Practices

<Warning>
  Always double-check customer email addresses and invoice amounts before sending to avoid payment delays.
</Warning>

### Professional Invoice Tips
- Include clear item descriptions
- Use professional language
- Set reasonable payment terms
- Include your contact information
- Add late payment policies

### Getting Paid Faster
- Send invoices immediately after work completion
- Include multiple payment options
- Set up automatic payment reminders
- Offer early payment discounts
- Use clear, simple language
