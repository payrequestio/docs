## Adding Customers

### Manual Entry
Add customers individually with complete information:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Basic Information">
    Enter customer name, email, and phone number
  </Step>
  <Step title="Billing Details">
    Add billing address and company information
  </Step>
  <Step title="Payment Preferences">
    Set preferred payment methods and terms
  </Step>
  <Step title="Custom Information">
    Add any custom fields relevant to your business
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Import Customers
Bulk import from existing systems:

- **CSV Upload**: Import from spreadsheets or other systems
- **Integration Sync**: Connect with CRM or accounting software
- **API Import**: Programmatically add customers
- **Data Validation**: Automatic verification of customer information

## Customer Segmentation

### Automatic Segmentation
PayRequest automatically categorizes customers:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Payment Behavior">
    - **Fast Payers**: Pay within terms consistently
    - **Reliable Payers**: Pay on time with occasional delays
    - **Slow Payers**: Frequently pay late but eventually pay
    - **Problem Payers**: Require frequent follow-up
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Business Value">
    - **High Value**: Large or frequent invoices
    - **Regular Customers**: Consistent business
    - **Occasional Customers**: Infrequent invoices
    - **New Customers**: Recently added to database
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Custom Segments
Create your own customer categories:

- **Industry Types**: Law firms, contractors, retailers, etc.
- **Geographic Regions**: Local, national, international
- **Service Types**: Different services you provide
- **Customer Size**: Small business, enterprise, individual

## Data Management

### Data Protection
Keep customer information secure:

<Warning>
  Always comply with data protection regulations like GDPR when storing and processing customer information.
</Warning>

- **Encryption**: All customer data is encrypted at rest and in transit
- **Access Controls**: Limit who can view and edit customer information
- **Data Retention**: Automatic compliance with retention policies
- **Privacy Settings**: Respect customer privacy preferences

### Data Export
Export customer information when needed:

- **CSV Export**: Spreadsheet format for analysis
- **PDF Reports**: Professional customer summaries
- **Integration Export**: Sync with CRM or accounting systems
- **Backup Options**: Regular data backups
