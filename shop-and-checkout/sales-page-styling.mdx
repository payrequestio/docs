# Sales Page Styling

Customize your sales page to reflect your brand identity. The Styling tab provides a live preview that updates in real-time as you make changes, so you can see exactly how your page will look to visitors.

## Overview

Your sales page is your public-facing profile where customers can browse your products and make purchases. The Styling tab lets you personalize:

- **Profile Avatar** - Your photo or logo
- **Name & Tagline** - Your business identity
- **Bio/Description** - Tell visitors about yourself
- **Social Links** - Connect your social profiles

<Tip>
Changes in the Styling tab update the preview in real-time. No need to save to see how it looks!
</Tip>

## Accessing the Styling Tab

1. Navigate to **Sales Page** from the main menu
2. Click the **Styling** tab (between Dynamic Links and Settings)
3. The page displays a two-column layout:
   - **Left**: Live preview of your sales page
   - **Right**: Styling options and controls

## Live Preview

The left column shows a real-time mockup of your sales page. This preview:

- Updates instantly as you type
- Shows your avatar, name, tagline, and social links
- Displays your selected products
- Mirrors the dark theme of your actual sales page

<Info>
The preview stays fixed while you scroll through the styling options, so you always see your changes.
</Info>

To see your actual live page, click **View Live Page** button to open it in a new tab.

## Profile Avatar

Your avatar appears prominently at the top of your sales page. You have two options:

### Option 1: Use Social Profile Avatar

If you've added social links, PayRequest can automatically fetch your avatar from those platforms:

| Platform | How It Works |
|----------|--------------|
| X (Twitter) | Uses your X profile picture |
| GitHub | Uses your GitHub avatar |
| YouTube | Uses your channel avatar |
| Twitch | Uses your Twitch profile picture |
| Substack | Uses your Substack author photo |
| Telegram | Uses your Telegram profile picture |
| SoundCloud | Uses your SoundCloud avatar |
| Website | Uses your website's favicon |

Simply click on any available avatar to select it. A blue checkmark indicates your current selection.

### Option 2: Upload Custom Avatar

1. Scroll to **"Or upload your own"** section
2. Drag and drop an image, or click to browse
3. Supported formats: JPG, PNG (up to 10MB)

<Tip>
For best results, use a square image at least 200x200 pixels.
</Tip>

### Removing Your Avatar

Click the **Remove** button next to your current avatar to revert to the default placeholder.

## Tagline & Bio

### Your Name / Business Name

This is the main heading on your sales page. It appears large and prominent below your avatar.

- Use your personal name for freelancer pages
- Use your company name for business pages
- Keep it concise and recognizable

### Tagline

A short subtitle that describes what you do. Examples:

- "Creator of Laravel"
- "Web Development & Design"
- "Premium Coffee Roasters"
- "Digital Marketing Expert"

<Note>
Your tagline appears directly below your name in a lighter color. Keep it under 50 characters for best display.
</Note>

### Description

A longer bio that tells visitors about you or your business. This appears below your social links and before your products.

Tips for a good description:
- Explain what you offer
- Highlight your expertise or unique value
- Keep it 2-3 sentences max
- Include a call to action if appropriate

## Social Links

Social links serve two purposes:

1. **Display on your sales page** - Visitors can click to visit your profiles
2. **Avatar selection** - PayRequest fetches avatars from these platforms

### Available Platforms

**Primary platforms** (always visible):
- Twitter/X
- GitHub
- YouTube
- LinkedIn
- Website

**Additional platforms** (click "More platforms" to expand):
- Twitch
- Substack
- Telegram
- SoundCloud

### Adding Social Links

1. Enter the full URL (e.g., `https://x.com/username`)
2. Or just your username - PayRequest will format it
3. Links update the avatar options automatically

<Warning>
Only add links to profiles you actively maintain. Broken links create a poor impression.
</Warning>

## Saving Your Changes

Click **Save Styling** at the bottom of the right column to save all your changes.

<Tip>
While the preview updates in real-time, your changes aren't saved until you click the save button. The actual live page only updates after saving.
</Tip>

## Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Use a professional, high-quality avatar">
    Your avatar is the first thing visitors see. Use a clear, well-lit photo or a clean logo. Avoid blurry images or complex graphics that don't scale well.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Keep your tagline focused">
    Your tagline should instantly communicate what you do. Avoid jargon or overly clever phrases. Clear and simple wins.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Only include active social profiles">
    Quality over quantity. It's better to have 2-3 active profiles than 8 dormant ones. Remove links to profiles you no longer use.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Write your description for your audience">
    Think about who visits your sales page. What do they need to know? Focus on benefits and outcomes, not just features.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Test on mobile">
    After saving, visit your sales page on your phone to ensure everything looks good on smaller screens.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Why can't I see some avatar options?">
    Avatar options only appear for platforms where you've entered a valid social link. Add your social profile URLs first, then the avatar options will become available.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I change my sales page URL?">
    The URL handle is configured in the **Settings** tab, not the Styling tab. Go to Settings and update the "Sales Page Handle" field.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I use custom colors or fonts?">
    The sales page uses a consistent dark theme for all users. For advanced customization, see [Custom CSS](/advanced-features/custom-css).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Why doesn't my avatar update immediately on the live page?">
    The preview updates in real-time, but the actual live page only updates after you click **Save Styling**. Make sure to save your changes.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What size should my avatar be?">
    Avatars are displayed at 96x96 pixels on desktop. Upload an image at least 200x200 pixels for crisp display on high-resolution screens.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
