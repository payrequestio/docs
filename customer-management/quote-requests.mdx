# Quote Requests 💬

Receive and manage quote requests directly from your freelancer page. Perfect for service-based businesses, consultants, and freelancers who want to engage with potential customers before creating formal invoices or proposals.

<Info>
**Freelancer Feature**: Quote Requests are available on all plans, including the Freelancer plan. Enable the quote button on your freelancer page to start receiving inquiries from potential customers.
</Info>

## 🎯 What Are Quote Requests?

Quote Requests allow visitors to your freelancer page to easily contact you about services, projects, or custom work. Instead of forcing every visitor into a payment flow, you can give them an option to request a custom quote first.

## 🚀 Setting Up Quote Requests

### Enable Quote Button on Your Freelancer Page

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Payment Page Settings">
    Go to **Settings** → **Payment Page** in your PayRequest dashboard
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Freelancer Page Type">
    Choose **Freelancer** as your page type to enable the freelancer showcase layout
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enable Quote Button">
    Scroll to the **Services & Products** section and toggle **"Enable Request Quote button"**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save Your Settings">
    Click **Save** to apply your changes. The quote button will now appear on your freelancer page
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
**Design Tip**: The quote button appears below your products/services showcase, making it easy for visitors to request custom quotes without being forced into immediate purchases.
</Tip>

## 📝 How Quote Requests Work

### Customer Experience

When a visitor clicks "Request Custom Quote" on your freelancer page:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Modal Opens">
    A clean, professional form appears as a modal overlay without leaving the page
  </Step>

  <Step title="Customer Fills Form">
    **Required Fields:**
    - Full name
    - Email address
    - Message describing what they need
  </Step>

  <Step title="Submit Request">
    Customer clicks "Send Request" - the button shows "Sending..." while processing
  </Step>

  <Step title="Success Confirmation">
    A green success callout appears: "Quote request sent! We'll get back to you as soon as possible"
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
**Clean URLs**: The quote form uses AJAX submission, so the URL stays clean without any query parameters. This provides a professional user experience.
</Info>

## 💼 Managing Quote Requests

### Viewing Requests

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Support Requests">
    Click **Support Requests** in your sidebar under Store Management
  </Step>

  <Step title="Filter by Type">
    Use the type filter to show only "Quote Request" entries
  </Step>

  <Step title="View Request Details">
    Click on any request to see the full conversation and customer information
  </Step>

  <Step title="Respond to Customer">
    Reply directly from the request detail page or use the customer's email from the notification
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Request Information

Each quote request includes:

<Expandable title="📋 Customer Information">
**Automatically Captured:**
- Customer name
- Email address (verified format)
- Request message
- Submission timestamp
- Source: "Freelancer Page"

**Customer Profile:**
- Automatically creates customer record if new
- Links to existing customer if email matches
- Maintains full customer history
- Enables future quote tracking
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="🏷️ Request Details">
**System Information:**
- **Type**: Quote Request
- **Status**: Open (by default)
- **Priority**: Medium
- **Subject**: "Quote Request from [Customer Name]"
- **Metadata**: Source tracking, customer details

**Management Options:**
- Change status (Open → In Review → Resolved/Rejected)
- Assign to team member
- Add internal notes
- Link to created invoices or products
</Expandable>

## 📊 Quote Request Workflow

### Typical Workflow

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Initial Contact">
    **Step 1: Customer Inquiry**
    1. Customer visits your freelancer page
    2. Browses your services/products
    3. Clicks "Request Custom Quote"
    4. Fills out the form with their needs
    5. Receives immediate confirmation

    **Your Action:**
    - Receive email notification
    - View request in dashboard
    - Review customer needs
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Follow-Up">
    **Step 2: Quote Preparation**
    1. Review the request details
    2. Assess scope and requirements
    3. Calculate pricing
    4. Prepare professional quote
    5. Respond via email or create invoice

    **System Support:**
    - Customer profile already created
    - Email history maintained
    - Easy invoice creation for customer
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Conversion">
    **Step 3: Close the Deal**
    1. Send quote via email or create invoice
    2. Customer approves and is ready to proceed
    3. Create invoice or subscription in PayRequest
    4. Link to the original quote request
    5. Mark request as "Resolved"

    **Benefits:**
    - Complete customer journey tracked
    - Historical reference maintained
    - Customer relationship established
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## 🛠️ Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Quote button not appearing on my page">
    **Solutions:**
    - Verify page type is set to "Freelancer"
    - Check that "Enable Request Quote button" is toggled ON
    - Clear your browser cache and refresh
    - Ensure you've saved your payment page settings
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Not receiving email notifications">
    **Check:**
    - Verify your email address in business settings
    - Check spam/junk folder
    - Ensure email notifications are enabled
    - Contact support if issue persists
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Customer says they can't submit the form">
    **Verify:**
    - All required fields are filled
    - Email address is valid format
    - Internet connection is stable
    - No browser extensions blocking the form
    - Try different browser if issue persists
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Quote requests not showing in dashboard">
    **Check:**
    - Navigate to Support Requests (not just Requests)
    - Clear any active filters
    - Refresh the page
    - Verify request type is "Quote Request"
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
