# Welcome to PayRequest

PayRequest is a comprehensive payment platform that enables businesses to collect payments online, send payment requests, and manage their financial workflows with ease. Our platform provides powerful APIs, intuitive interfaces, and seamless integrations to help you get paid faster.

## What is PayRequest?

PayRequest simplifies payment collection by offering:

- **Payment Links**: Generate secure payment links that can be shared via email, SMS, or embedded in your applications
- **Payment Requests**: Send professional payment requests to customers with automated follow-ups
- **Online Checkout**: Integrate our checkout experience into your website or application
- **Subscription Management**: Handle recurring payments and subscription billing
- **Multi-Payment Methods**: Support for credit cards, bank transfers, digital wallets, and local payment methods
