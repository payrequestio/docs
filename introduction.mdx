# Welcome to PayRequest

PayRequest is a comprehensive payment platform that enables businesses to create professional invoices, collect payments online, and manage their financial workflows with ease. Our platform provides intuitive tools and seamless processes to help you get paid faster.

## What is PayRequest?

PayRequest simplifies payment collection by offering:

- **Professional Invoices**: Create and send beautiful, branded invoices to your customers
- **Payment Links**: Generate secure payment links that can be shared via email, SMS, or embedded in your applications
- **Payment Requests**: Send payment requests to customers with automated follow-ups and reminders
- **Online Checkout**: Offer your customers a seamless payment experience
- **Multi-Payment Methods**: Support for credit cards, bank transfers, digital wallets, and local payment methods
